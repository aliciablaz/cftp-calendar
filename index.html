Code for the People is a documentary short film about the open web, open source, and digital ownership, directed by Bao Nguyen and presented by Automattic. The film premieres July 1 at Crosby Street Hotel in NYC, followed by a screening at KQED in San Francisco (July 8 or 15), with a global digital release on July 9 at codeforthepeople.com.

The Creative Lab / Design Core team is responsible for all visual materials powering the posts in this calendar — social avatars, poster variations, quote card templates, Instagram Story templates, video overlays, motion bumpers, and a Figma/Canva playground for ongoing asset creation. Design work is tracked in Linear DSGCLAB-862. The creative approach mirrors the care and thoughtfulness of the 20th anniversary and Automattic HQ projects.
